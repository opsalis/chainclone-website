2. Description of Service

ChainClone provides blockchain state migration services for EVM-compatible chains. The Service reads publicly available on-chain data (contract bytecode, storage state, account balances, and token ownership records) from a source chain and deploys equivalent state on a destination chain specified by the Customer.

The Service does not migrate transaction history, event logs, or off-chain data. Migrated contracts receive new addresses on the destination chain.
